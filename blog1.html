Places to visit mysore in one day

Mysore is the city of silk and sandalwood.Although it is not a big city it has a lot of places to cover. one must visit place is the chamundi hills located 8km from the city of mysore. If one wants to admire royalty then mysore palace is the place, if one visits in the evening then one can notice lights in the palace that looks like a golden crown of mysore city. Also other than that ooty and coorg is very near to mysore which is around 130km.The best part is to visit mysore in the time of dasara festival. Do visit this beautiful place at least for once.

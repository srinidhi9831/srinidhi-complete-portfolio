Places to visit mysore in one day

Mysore is a city which retains a lot of old world charm. Mysore is the city of silk and sandalwood. There are some areas in the city which seem to have been caught in a time warp, as if time has stood still and life goes on in a leisurely and unhurried pace. However things are changing and Mysore too is changing gears. But, one can still bask in the old world elegance even today. Mysore which is now officially known as Mysuru is a city situated in Karnataka a Southern State of India. It is about 145 Kilometers by road from the State capital of Bangalore. The city has a rich history and culture and is known for its magnificent palaces and the pomp and gaiety of the festivities that take place during the festival of Dasara. The festivities are marked by royal splendor and colorful programs with the city brightly decorated for a grand procession.
